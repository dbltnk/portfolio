Past

In 2009 I co-founded my first game-dev studio, the Bit Barons. Before that I graduated in Media Management (Public Relations) from MHMK and got my Diploma in Online Marketing from dda. Before that I fixed computers.

Present

I'm currently working on Das Tal, a time-boxed PvP sandbox MMORPG for the PC. Besides that I'm organizing events like the Game Design Dojo and I serve as the regional coordinator of Global Game Jam in Germany, Austria and Switzerland.

Future

I'm going to release a unique and sustainable sandbox MMO. I'll become a better designer and a decent gameplay coder. I'll do more talks at different events. I'd also like to have a pet squirrel. Get in touch if you've got more ideas.
