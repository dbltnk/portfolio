Interests

I am interested in playful experiences that facilitate human interaction. I'm also intrigued by emergent social dynamics in complex systems with cooperative and competitive elements.

Major Things in Machine Learning

  • 2024/25: Helped write the European Union's Code of Practice for the AI Act
  • 2023/24: Helped set up the UK's AI Safety Institute
  • 2019–2023: Designed multi-agent research environments as an Environment Designer at DeepMind

Major Things in Video Games

  • Founded Fairytale Distillery (2013), designed the competitive online game The Exiled
  • Co-founded Bit Barons (2009)
  • Organized events like GameCamp Munich and Global Game Jam (2009–2018)
