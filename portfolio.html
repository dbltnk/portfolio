My Interests

I am interested in playful experiences that facilitate human interaction. I'm also intrigued by emergent social dynamics in complex systems with cooperative and competitive elements.

Machine Learning

In 2024/25 helped write the European Union's Code of Practice for the AI Act. In 2023/24 helped set up the UK's AI Safety Institute. From 2019 to 2023 I designed multi-agent research environments as an Environment Designer at DeepMind.

Video Games

I founded Fairytale Distillery in 2013 and designed the competitive online game The Exiled. Before that I co-founded the Bit Barons 2009 and organized events like GameCamp Munich and Global Game Jam from 2009 to 2018.
